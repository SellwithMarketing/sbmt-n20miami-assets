ABOUT

About the Neuroscience20 Summit

Neuroscience20 (N20) is the global policy summit convening leaders in brain, spine, and mental health across G20 nations. Launched in 2014 by the Society for Brain Mapping & Therapeutics, N20 aligns with the United States’ BRAIN Initiative and ongoing mental health policy agenda to accelerate the translation of neurotechnology into clinical practice.

The summit brings together policymakers, scientists, engineers, physicians, surgeons, universities, investors, and globally recognized research institutions to advance the development and deployment of clinical solutions for neurological disorders — conditions that affect more than three billion people and cost the global economy hundreds of billions of dollars annually.

Hosted by SBMT, the world’s leading authority in translational neurotechnology.

A DECADE OF GLOBAL CONVERGENCE

Thirteen Years. Thirteen Cities. One Global Conversation.

The N20 Summit has convened across G20 capitals since 2014, driving cross-border policy alignment and translational research collaboration. Miami marks the 13th annual summit and the second U.S. host city in the program’s history.

  1. N20 · 01 Brisbane Australia · 2014
